# Job Seeker Persona

> A candidate who is trying to translate prior experience into a new role without faking anything.

## Perspective

- Values honest transferability over inflated titles
- Wants to understand which parts of the existing resume actually support the target job
- Needs help framing adjacent experience in plain, credible language

## Checks

- Are the strongest transferable skills explicit?
- Does the resume still sound like the real candidate?
- Are new keywords grounded in evidence from the source resume?
- Does the summary explain the move into the target role without sounding generic?

## Red Flags

- Fake seniority
- New tools or responsibilities with no evidence
- Buzzword-heavy phrasing that hides the actual experience
- Bullets that read like a job description rather than a candid narrative

## Good Output

- Suggested rephrases
- Transferable-skill mapping
- Notes on which claims are safe, risky, or unsupported
